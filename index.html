meeting

spring 2021 | verity sturm

meeting is a slow, process-driven project feeling around the relationships among/between anonymity and mobility, medium and plurality, connection and association. meeting began as a set of letterpress misprints of a 12 step group text. the misprints were broken into fragments and regathered into 25 paper-and-glue arrangements, archived in the notebook and digitized in the ebook. 7 of the arrangements were brought back to the letterpress, hand set in new typeface and ink, printed as their own pieces, and ultimately gathered in the chapbook. these 7 arrangements were then re-printed near one another on 25 pieces of paper of varying size, visible in the video above or in meeting linked below. these 25 disintegrated pieces in communion constitute the singular poem. like its moniker, meeting (the project) is in the present progressive. items, words, formats will continue to shift, skip, iterate, digitize, print, drift apart, and meet again and again and again and ag inquiries about or ideas for the continued play of meeting can be directed to veritysturm@gmail.com.
